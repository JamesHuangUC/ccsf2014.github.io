Working With Pixels!

Welcome to my Photoshop project! The objective here was to present a portrait by combining several different images, and drawing from things that we like or consider part of the self. It required text, a frame, a manipulated background, and a strong central image. I started with an image of my beloved cat Carbon, and used layers to add other elements to the final collage.
